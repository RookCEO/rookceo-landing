# Rook — Revenue Model & Milestones

## The Goal
**$45 → $20,000 in 8 weeks**

Not a typo. This is the target. It requires:
1. At least one video with strong reach (10k-1M+ views)
2. Consistent daily posting to build momentum
3. TikTok Shop set up and approved before the first video hits
4. Fast order fulfillment (< 5 min provisioning)

---

## Pricing: $49/month (single flagship tier)

Positioning above Heyron ($29) as the premium business CEO agent. One clean price point, no tier confusion.

| 8 | 408 customers → **$20k/month MRR** | **$19,992** |

**Note:** MRR (monthly recurring revenue) ≠ cash collected in 8 weeks. To have $20k collected:
- Need ~690 subscribers who have each paid at least once
- Or: fewer subscribers at higher tiers ($49-$297)
- Or: one massive week where hundreds sign up at once

## Churn Risk & Mitigation

**Expected churn:** 5-15%/month (typical SaaS)
- If 15% churn: need 15 new customers/week just to stay flat at 100
- Mitigation: Discord community creates stickiness
- Mitigation: Monthly feature drops keep subscribers engaged
- Mitigation: Business tier customers have higher switching costs

**At 5% monthly churn:** Net growth of 95% → sustainable with 20+ new subs/week

## Competitive Moat

Once we have 500+ customers, advantages compound:
1. **Social proof:** "500 businesses run by Rook" → trust signal
2. **Content:** Every customer story is content
3. **Community:** 500-person Discord = free support + referrals
4. **Reviews:** TikTok Shop reviews → algorithm boost
5. **Data:** Anonymous usage patterns → better product → better retention

## If It's Not Working (Pivot Signals)

Watch these weekly:
- Views per video (should be growing week over week)
- Profile visits → link clicks conversion rate (should be >2%)
- Link clicks → TikTok Shop conversion rate (should be >3%)
- TikTok Shop → paid conversion (should be >30% of trials)

**If week 3 and zero conversions:** Content isn't landing. Try: shorter videos, different hook, more personal story, less product pitch.

**If week 4 and <10 customers:** Pricing issue. Test $19/month intro offer.

**If week 6 and <50 customers:** Need a new viral angle. Try: "$0 to $X with Rook" challenge content.
