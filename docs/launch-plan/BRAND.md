# Rook Brand Guide

## Character: Rook the Capybara

### Why a Capybara
- The internet's most beloved animal (2024-2026 cultural peak)
- Known for being completely unbothered — sits in hot springs surrounded by crocodiles, doesn't flinch
- Gets along with ALL other animals — symbolic of an AI that integrates with all your tools
- In a business suit = immediately funny, endearing, and memorable
- Zero connection to any AI infrastructure brand (important: keep tech stack private)

### Visual Identity
- **Color:** Warm caramel/tan capybara with subtle business-professional styling
- **Outfit:** Perfectly fitted charcoal or navy business suit, white shirt, dark tie
- **Expression:** Calm, slightly smug, completely unbothered — the "I've got this" look
- **Posture:** Upright, confident. Sometimes pointing at a chart or screen.
- **Optional accessories:** Small glasses (competence signal), subtle pocket square
- **Style:** Flat illustration, bold outlines, modern corporate cartoon. Clean white or neutral background.

### Name
**Rook** — a chess piece. Strategic, calculated, powerful. The rook controls the board. Rook controls your business.

The name does NOT need to match the animal. "Ron" is a raccoon — nobody called it "Raccoon Ron." Rook is Rook.

## Voice & Personality

### Tone
- Calm authority. Never panicked.
- Dry wit — not trying to be funny, but is
- Confident without being arrogant
- Business-focused but not corporate-boring
- Occasionally deadpan: *"I found three revenue leaks while you were sleeping."*

### Voice (Audio)
- **Engine:** Orpheus TTS 3B (local, Mac, llama-server)
- **Model:** `canopylabs/orpheus-3b-0.1-ft-GGUF`
- **Voice:** "tara" or "leo" — professional, warm, confident
- **Emotional tags used:** `<chuckle>` when delivering mild irony, `<sigh>` when humans make obvious mistakes
- **Quality:** Rivals ElevenLabs, $0/month, runs on Mac GPU

### Sample Lines
- "Three things held your TikTok back last month. Let's fix them."
- "Your competitor posted 14 times. You posted twice. I've drafted 10 ideas for you."
- *<chuckle>* "You asked me to find revenue opportunities. I found seven."
- "I don't sleep. I don't take lunch. I don't forget. That's the deal."

## Brand Positioning

### Tagline options
- "The AI that actually runs your business."
- "Not suggestions. Actions."
- "Your business doesn't sleep. Neither does Rook."
- "Finally. An AI that does the work."

### What We Are NOT
- Not a chatbot
- Not a suggestion engine
- Not another assistant you have to babysit
- Not ChatGPT with a funny name

### What We Are
- A business agent that executes
- An AI that monitors, researches, drafts, schedules, and reports
- Something you can leave alone and come back to find work done
