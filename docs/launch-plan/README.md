# Rook — The AI That Actually Runs Your Business

**Product:** Rook is a personal AI business agent that does real work autonomously — not just suggestions or chat. It monitors your email, runs scheduled tasks, researches competitors, drafts content, and sends you daily briefings. You ask, it does.

**Mascot:** Rook the Capybara — a calm, unbothered capybara in a business suit. The capybara is the internet's most beloved animal, known for getting along with everyone and never stressing. That's Rook.

**Business model:** $49/month hosted SaaS — one premium tier ("Rook CEO"), full capability from day one. Customers get their own private Rook instance. Custom tier at $297/month for done-for-you configuration.

**TikTok channel:** The marketing engine. Andrew (human face) documents what Rook does, Rook's autonomous decisions, and Rook's "money schemes." Each video ends with a CTA to get your own Rook.

**Status:** Pre-launch. Building toward first TikTok video → waitlist → TikTok Shop launch.
