# TikTok Shop Setup

## Entity
**DataNova Consulting LLC** — use LLC EIN throughout. Do NOT use personal SSN.

## Application URL
https://seller-us.tiktok.com/account/register

## Required Documents

### For Corporate seller (DataNova Consulting LLC):
- [ ] Business name: DataNova Consulting LLC
- [ ] EIN (Employer Identification Number)
- [ ] Registered business address
- [ ] Last 4 digits of tax ID (EIN, not SSN)
- [ ] SS-4 IRS confirmation letter OR EIN verification letter
- [ ] W9 form (download blank at irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/fw9.pdf)
- [ ] Business bank account (routing + account number)
- [ ] Merchant category: Software / Digital Services / Subscriptions

## Key Facts (Confirmed from TikTok FAQ)
- **No follower requirement** — can sell with 0 followers
- **Approval time:** 1-3 business days
- **Cost:** Free to set up, TikTok takes a small commission on sales
- Can sell digital services and software subscriptions
