## The Clear Winner: rookceo.com at $11.28/yr

**Why rookceo.com beats everything:**
- 7 characters: short, memorable, typeable
- "Rook CEO" = the capybara CEO narrative in the domain name
- Tells the story: Rook is the CEO (of your business)
- .com = trust, authority, no explanation needed
- Under $12/year = negligible cost even if this doesn't work out
- Available right now

**How to register:** namecheap.com with promo code `NEWCOM679` for ~$6.79 first year

---

## If going premium: getrook.ai ($85/yr)
The .ai TLD signals "this is real AI tech" to a tech-savvy audience. But:
- TikTok's audience skews non-technical small business owners
- .com converts better than .ai for general audiences
- $85 vs $11 = 7x the cost with uncertain upside
- Recommendation: **Start with rookceo.com, upgrade to getrook.ai if/when revenue justifies**
