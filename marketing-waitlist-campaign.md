# Rook CEO Waitlist Campaign

Landing page: https://rookceo.com/start.html
Primary CTA: Join the waitlist
Audience: SMB founders/operators losing time to messy workflows, manual admin, disconnected tools, and AI hype without execution.
Offer angle: A private Rook agent for operators: secure, preconfigured, and useful out of the box. Users enter goals, and the agent starts working on them through reminders, research, follow-ups, task coordination, recurring jobs, and workflow automation.
Internal architecture note: Rook CEO may use Hermes-agent-level capabilities under the hood, on a private VPS with Tailscale hardening, orchestrator behavior, Kanban jobs, scheduled automations, memory, and tool access. Do not use “Hermes Agent” or “OpenClaw” in end-user marketing copy. Sell the branded Rook CEO agent and outcomes.

## Positioning

**One-liner:** Rook CEO gives small-business operators a private Rook agent that can remember context, run jobs, coordinate tasks, and start working from the goals they enter.

**Core hook:** Give Rook CEO a goal. It starts moving the work forward.

**Promise:** Join the waitlist for a secure, preconfigured AI operator that helps turn goals into research, follow-ups, recurring jobs, workflow fixes, and practical business execution without enterprise bloat.

## Campaign Goal

Drive waitlist signups for Rook CEO early access.

Target conversion path:
1. Short post/video hook names a painful workflow leak.
2. CTA sends to `rookceo.com/start.html`.
3. Signup enters ERPNext waitlist with source `rookceo-start-email` or `rookceo-start-google`.
4. Waitlist entries can be reviewed, qualified, updated, or deleted from ERPNext by the operator when needed.
