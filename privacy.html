Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Rook CEO ("we", "us", or "our") operates rookceo.com. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Sign-up: your name and email address when you create an account or join the waitlist.
  • Communications: any messages you send to us via email.

We also collect limited information automatically when you visit the site:

  • Page views and referral source via Umami Analytics (self-hosted, cookieless, no cross-site tracking).

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your Rook CEO account.
  • To notify you when your access is ready or when important updates occur.
  • To respond to questions or requests you send us.
  • To understand how people discover and use the site so we can improve it.

We do not sell your information. We do not share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Integrations

Rook CEO connects to third-party platforms on your behalf when you authorize us to do so. These include but are not limited to TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and Threads. When you connect a platform account:

  • We access only the permissions you explicitly grant.
  • We use those permissions solely to perform the actions you request (e.g. scheduling and publishing content).
  • You can revoke access at any time from within the Rook CEO dashboard or directly from the third-party platform's settings.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by emailing rook@rookceo.com.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at rook@rookceo.com.

Cookies

This site does not use tracking cookies. Our analytics tool (Umami) is cookieless and does not collect personally identifiable information.

Children's Privacy

Rook CEO is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.
